Kazi Narzul Islam

The national poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is especially recognized as Birodhi Kobi(rebel poet) around the country. Narzul is a famous poet, musician and a philosopher of Bangladesh. The famous poet Narzul's writings deal with the revolution of that period of British government. His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh. He has raised his voice against any kinds of fascism and opression and got popularity swiftly around the Indian Subcontinent.

Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writings explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity and revolution. He opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism, including religious, caste-based and gender-based. Throughout his career, Nazrul worte short stories, novels and esays but is best known for his songs and poems. He pioneered new music forms such as Bengali ghazals. Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs(many recorded on HMV and gramophone records), collectively known as Nazrul Geeti.

"Love has no meaning or amount."-Kazi Nazrul Islam
